PERSONAL CARE SERVICES

We believe that the relief one feels being at their own home is important for them and their well-being. We are committed on providing high quality, compassionate, considerate and respectful care for your loved one with the respect, love and kindness they deserve. Alternative Home Care Services Company offers the followings Personal Care Services:

  • Transferring Clients
  • Grooming and Dressing
  • Meal preparation and feeding
  • Oral hygiene and denture care
  • Toileting and toilet hygiene
  • Bathing
  • Taking Temperatures and Vitals
  • Administering emergency and first aid
  • Assistance with Self-administration of medications by:
    • Reminding the client to take medications
    • Opening containers for the clients

COMPANIONSHIP

Your elderly loved ones deserve to have someone that provides in home companionship. A considerate and trustworthy companion that will help your loved one stay socially engaged with regular visits to the home. With our reliable caregivers, we guarantee that our clients are in good hands and will enjoy their day. Our services include:

  • Planning daily schedule
  • Scheduling appointments
  • Appointment remainders
  • Games and recreation
  • Assisiting them on their daily activities
  • Reading and Listening
  • Reviewing their daily activities
  • Birthday reminders
  • Take walks together
  • Discuss current events
  • Rent and watch movies
